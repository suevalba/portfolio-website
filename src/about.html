My Story

I have a strong background in customer service. Just like in UX Design, I must always listen to what customers need first. I listen to their story to establish empathy and then figure out for them what really the problem is. Customers loves to express their frustration instead of the problem. I’ve learned to communicate with them in a way that they would understand. Sometimes, I don’t have the answers to their problems so with the help of my team managers, supervisors and coaches, we can produce solutions on how to help them better.
